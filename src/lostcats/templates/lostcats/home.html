LostCat is a street art phenomenon in Ithaca, New York

This site is a community-sourced map and gallery of LostCats. Please contribute any new cats you find.

See the cats on our map
On the issues
  • The artist is anonymous, so all graphics here are uncredited. It adds to the mystery.
  • We support the cats. Most LostCats stay off private property and hide in small spaces. They are nice.
  • We do not encourage CopyCats. Just enjoy the cats we already have.
  • We aren't profiting from the art, but we may eventually try to cover our server costs somehow.

- the LostCat team
